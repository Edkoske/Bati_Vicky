About Bati Vicky Chepkorir

A Voice for Every Woman in Kericho County

Bati Vicky Chepkorir is a dedicated community leader, advocate for women's rights, and a passionate believer in the power of inclusive governance. Born and raised in Kericho County, Bati understands the unique challenges facing our communities and has committed her life to service and empowerment.

With a background in community development and years of grassroots engagement, Bati has worked tirelessly to uplift marginalized voices, champion education for our children, and create economic opportunities for women and youth across our county.

Her vision is rooted in integrity, transparency, and a deep commitment to ensuring that every voice in Kericho County is heard and represented. As your County Woman Representative, she pledges to work alongside you to build a future where no one is left behind.

Why I Am Running

I am running for County Woman Representative because I believe in the power of unity and the strength of our community. For too long, many voices in Kericho County have gone unheard, and too many opportunities have passed our people by.

I have walked alongside you in our villages, listened to your concerns at market places, and witnessed the resilience of our mothers, sisters, and daughters who work tirelessly to provide for their families despite numerous challenges.

This campaign is not about me‚Äîit is about us. It is about creating a Kericho County where every woman has access to healthcare, where every child can pursue education without barriers, where our youth find meaningful employment, and where our elderly are cared for with dignity.
